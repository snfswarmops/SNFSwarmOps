SwarmOps is an SNSF-funded project on AI-enabled Cyber-Physical Systems (AI-CPSs), focusing on **human-sensing based MLOps** to support **collaborative cyber-physical systems**.

**Quick facts**
- **Grant:** SNSF Project **219732**
- **Title/Subitle:** SwarmOps — *Human-sensing based MLOps for Collaborative Cyber-Physical Systems*
- **Duration:** **2025–2029**
- **PI:** **Sebastiano Panichella**
- **Approved amount:** **667,280 CHF**
- **Status:** Running / Ongoing
